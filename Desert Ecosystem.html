Desert Ecosystem



A desert ecosystem is a type of ecosystem that is characterized by a low amount of rainfall, high temperatures, and sparse vegetation. Deserts are typically found in regions that receive less than 10 inches of precipitation each year, and they can be found in every continent on Earth. The organisms that live in desert ecosystems have adapted to the harsh conditions, such as by conserving water and tolerating extreme temperatures. Despite the challenging conditions, desert ecosystems can be incredibly diverse and home to a wide range of unique species.
